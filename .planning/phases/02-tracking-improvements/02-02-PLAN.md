<objective>
Upgrade the HSV re-ID system in AdvancedFeetDetector to use k-means k=3 color clustering for the gallery appearance descriptor, add a jersey-number tiebreaker in `_reid()` for ambiguous match cases, and call `buf.reset_slot()` inside `_activate_slot()` on slot eviction to prevent stale jersey votes carrying over to new occupants.

Purpose: Reduce ID switches on games where two teams wear similar primary colors (e.g. BOS green vs MIL green). The tiebreaker uses confirmed jersey numbers from the OCR voting buffer when appearance distance is within a tie band. The reset_slot() call prevents a known pitfall (RESEARCH.md Pitfall 3) where a re-entering player inherits stale votes from a previous occupant of the same slot.
Output: Updated src/tracking/advanced_tracker.py with REID_TIE_BAND, enhanced _compute_appearance, tiebreaker in _reid, reset_slot call in _activate_slot
